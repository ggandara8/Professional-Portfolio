About Me

My name is Gerardo Gándara and I’m a Full Stack Developer. I create web pages and hopefully apps soon. I call Fort Worth, Texas home but I’m originally from Carolina, Puerto Rico. I consider myself a hard worker and a creative Developer and I thoroughly enjoy the creative and problem solving aspect of being a developer. Originally I went to school for Sports Management and I’ve been working within Customer Service for the past 10+ years. With this portfolio I want to share a little bit of my work and I truly hope you can admire and enjoy what I’ve created. For any questions or inquiries please don’t hesitate to reach out.
